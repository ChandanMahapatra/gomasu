About

Overview

Development of society and its underprivileged people has been a challenge, more so, in India. In this direction, many a efforts have been made and are going on still.

A few community oriented people led by a committed Gandhian, Co-operative Banker, Homeopath and Social worker Dr. Mahadev Mahapatra started formalizing further community related activities. This gave birth to Gomasu Development Foundation in 2009.

Picture: Mahadev Mahapatra

"Potential Expressions through Creativity"

Mission

Picture: Mahadev Mahapatra

Gomasu Development Foundation, having been registered as a charitable institution, has very lofty objectives. As envisioned, it is going to emphasise Education and Health for enabling holistic development of human beings in society with focus on the process “Potential Expression through Creativity.”

Service Initiatives

Who do we help?

We help economically weaker,mentally/health challenged and underprivileged children whose life will experience accelerated growth with encouragement and support. This helps in building their confidence and self esteem to become valuable citizens in society.

What do we do?

We focus our activities on facilitating development in the areas of educational scholarship, awards for talented children in arts, studies, health awareness and wholesome development of individuals; more so, the economic Ali and socially underprivileged strata of the society in India.
