Volunteer

Why volunteer?

We help underprivileged excel in life and try to bring creativity into their lives through art competitions.

Scholarships are also given to students to help them afford books and other necessary items for their basic education needs.

Help is always welcome!

Volunteers usually help in organizing events. They also assist in managing the NGO.
