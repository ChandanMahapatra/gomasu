Get Involved

Volunteer

Why volunteer?

We help underprivileged excel in life and try to bring creativity into their lives through art competitions.

Scholarships are also given to students to help them afford books and other necessary items for their basic education needs.

Help is always welcome!

Volunteers usually help in organizing events. They also assist in managing the NGO.

If this sounds perfect for you or if there is any other way you want to help contact us.

Donate

Why donate?

Your contributions to Gomasu will directly go to the talented individuals who are economically disadvantaged and socially underprivileged.

These contributions will enable them for become more wholistic individuals in the society.
